About Me

Building with Purpose

I'm a web developer, graphic designer, and AI commercial creator from Nepal. I recently completed my Higher Secondary Education (+2) in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Computer Science, and I'm now preparing to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering — while actively taking on real design and development work.

My focus is helping businesses grow through technology and creative marketing: clean, fast websites; brand visuals that stand out; and commercial-style advertisements built with AI-assisted image and video tools. I enjoy the full range of the process — from strategy and concept to final, polished delivery.

I'm a believer in starting small and thinking big — continuous learning, innovative problem-solving, and building a portfolio that reflects both creativity and technical craft.

⚡ Fun Fact: I'm equally comfortable writing a line of code and directing an AI-assisted commercial concept — I like sitting at the intersection of development, design, and advertising.
